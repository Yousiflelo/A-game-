How to Play

  • Click Start Game to begin. Watch the sequence of colors that lights up.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the color buttons in the same order.
  • Each correct sequence increases your score and advances you to the next level.
  • Each level adds one more color to the sequence, making it progressively harder.
  • Use hints sparingly - you only get 3 per game!
  • Choose your difficulty level: Easy (slow), Medium (normal), Hard (fast).
  • Try to beat your high score!
